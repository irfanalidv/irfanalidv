### A few things about my work

Most AI projects fail at the same places.<br>
Retrieval breaks on real data. Agents loop forever. Pipelines that passed evals fall over in production. Costs explode at scale.

That's where I spend my time — somewhere between **LLM orchestration**, **agentic pipelines**, and **production data engineering**. The full system from ingestion to deployment, not just the model layer.

Seven-plus years across data engineering, NLP, and LLM systems. I've shipped at a Schneider Electric company, built the entire AI layer at a Hong Kong startup, and now I'm building [Reflecta](https://www.getreflecta.com) and maintaining open-source AI infrastructure through [DataCortex IQ](https://www.datacortex.in).
